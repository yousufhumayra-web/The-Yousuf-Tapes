For me music is truly amazing. From Acid Jazz to NeoSoul and Hiphop to R&B. All these genres help paint a truly amazing story of struggle, hardship or love. It's meant to be relished, pondered over and debated over. This little corner of the web has some of my takes on the industries past and present releases.

About the Author

The Yousuf Tapes is a mini blog by Yousuf Choudhury. A teenager with a passion for understanding stories found within music. His passion was first rooted in learning instruments from a young age such as the piano, drums and steel pans. He started this project to share his passion for sound and help provide people his perspective on music and try gague the audiences perspective to greater projects.
